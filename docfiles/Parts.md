

****using CMOS chips for this project only*
# Parts needed
## Adder/Subtractor Circuit
- 6 NOT gates
- 22 AND gates
- 20 XOR gates
- 2 DIP switches (4 ins)
- 8 OR gates
- 1 DIP switch (1 in)
- 1 binary to BCD (atleast 5 bits)
- 1 BCD to 7 seg (at least 1's and 10's place)
- 2 7-seg displays (common cathode)
## Array Multiplier
### 3 bit version
- 18 AND gates
- 3 OR gates
- 9 XOR gates 
- 2 3 input DIP switches
- 1 Binary to BCD
- 2 BCD to 7-seg
- 2 7-seg
### 4 bit version
- 36 AND gates
- 20 XOR gates 
- 4 OR gates
- 2 4 input DIP switches
- 1 Binary to BCD
- 2 BCD to 7-seg
- 2 7-seg
